Kappacino Creations was originally created back in 2001 when I first began my work at an advertising agency. I was sort of muscled into learning Flash because I had taken an animation course at Kutztown. Not exactly the worst thing to be muscled into, I suppose.

It was originally a site to house my animations, featuring a fox named Kitsune and Atom Kappa, where the title of the site actually comes from. A Kappa is a japanese water monster. They're actually really nasty in mythology, but the Japanese have made them adorable.

Anyway, as you can see, the animations are still here, but this site has become more about featuring my comics and illustrations. A sort of web portal for my various art endeavors, I suppose.

The next natural question is: Who AM I?
Well, I'm Sara Miscavage and I like to draw stuff. Anime and cartoons are my specialty.

I'm a freelance illustrator from not-so-sunny Pennsylvania, and I live with my husband and two cats. One is gray and named Shady. The other is named Ghost. He is white, deaf and clinically insane. But I love him. Cats are awesome. But this isn't about cats. It's about art.

I've been doing art for as long as I can remember. I loved it so much that I went to college for it and I graduated in 2000 with a bachelor degree in fine arts.

After I graduated, I worked for 12 years at a full-service ad agency doing all things prepress, graphic design, illustration and animation. I learned quite a bit about design at my previous job, but after 12 long years, I decided that it was time for me to move forward and finally pursue a career in Illustration.

Throughout the years, I have had the opportunity to work with clients all over the world, including local clients like EPSCO as well as clients across the country, such as WeLoveFine. I hope to continue to do Illustration as my career as long as I can. It is truly my passion.

If you want to hire me, feel free to email me. I check my emails on a very regular basis. I will promise you this if you do decide to work on a project with me. I am fast and I am professional. And I promise I will do my absolute best on your project!
