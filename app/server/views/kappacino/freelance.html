What does that mean? I'm glad you asked! Here are some frequently asked questions about my freelance work:

What do you charge?
All of my projects are pretty much on a case by case basis because everything takes a different amount of time. If you want a quote, please email me and we will work out the details. Also, since i am a freelance illustrator, my schedule is unpredictable, so I reserve the right to turn down any work. It doesn't mean I hate you or am being malicious, it just means that sometimes things get busy. :)

Do you accept PayPal?
I sure do!

What sort of images won't you do?
Generally, if it isn't rated G or PG, I won't be able to take it on as a freelance project. If you are unsure what fits in these guidelines, please do not hesitate to ask me via email.

What about a large project? Will you be willing to do something like that?
Definitely email me on this. If my schedule isn't totally filled, it wouldn't be totally out of the realm of possibility. :)

I think that about wraps it up. If there is something here I didn't cover, please contact me via email. I hope to hear from you soon! ^_^
